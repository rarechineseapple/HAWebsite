About Hydro Aquatic

Founded in 2008, Hydro Aquatic has grown to become a leader in aquatic facility services. Our mission is to provide comprehensive, professional solutions that ensure safety, cleanliness, and enjoyment for all water-based facilities.

Our team consists of certified professionals with decades of combined experience in lifeguarding, pool maintenance, and construction. We pride ourselves on our attention to detail, commitment to safety, and customer-first approach.
