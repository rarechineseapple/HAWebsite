Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We’ve got answers. Whether you're planning an event, maintaining a pool, or hiring a lifeguard, we’re here to help.

How do I book an inflatable rental for an event?
Simply reach out to us via the Contact Page or email us directly. We’ll confirm availability, event details, and arrange setup and takedown. Early booking is recommended during school holidays and peak seasons.
Do I need to provide lifeguards for your inflatables?
For safety reasons, we always recommend having certified lifeguards on duty. We can bundle lifeguard staffing with your inflatable rental for seamless coverage.
What pool chemicals do you supply?
We supply chlorine, pH balancers, algaecides, shock treatments, and more — all trusted by institutions and certified safe for public use. Bulk ordering and delivery options are available.
Are your lifeguards certified?
Yes. All our lifeguards are certified and undergo continuous training to meet the latest safety protocols and local regulations. We also provide emergency response readiness and first-aid skills.
Can I request a custom pool setup or maintenance plan?
Absolutely. We offer customizable pool construction, maintenance schedules, and even themed event packages. Let us know your goals and we'll tailor a solution just for you.

Still have questions?

Reach out and our team will be happy to assist you directly.
